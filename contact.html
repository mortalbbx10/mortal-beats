Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a beat?

Browse the beats catalog, select the beat you like, choose your preferred license, and add it to your cart. Complete the checkout process, and you'll receive download links for your purchased beats.

What's the difference between license types?

Basic licenses allow for limited use, while Premium licenses offer more flexibility. Exclusive licenses transfer full rights to you, and the beat is removed from the store. Check the licensing page for detailed information.

Do you offer custom beats?

Yes! I create custom beats tailored to your specific needs. Contact me with details about your project, and we can discuss timeline and pricing.

How long does it take to receive my beats?

After purchase, you'll receive download links immediately. If you encounter any issues, please contact me, and I'll resolve them as quickly as possible.
